What are PPC Ads?

PPC is short for Pay Per Click. Advertisers pay a fee whenever their ads are clicked. Search engine advertising and other channels such as Facebook offer PPC ad models.

What social media platforms are best for my business?

For some, Facebook will be the best platform for business while Instagram or LinkedIn may be better for others. To determine which platform is best for your company, it is important to determine your target audience and social media goals.

What is Lead Generation?

Lead Generation is the initiation of consumer interest or enquiry into products or services of a business. In short, it's a way of identifying and cultivating potential customers.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is digital marketing?

    Digital marketing involves marketing to people using Internet-conneted electronic devices, namely computers, smartphones, and tablets. Digital marketing focuses on channels such as search engines, social media, email, websites, and apps to connect with prospects and customers.

  • Why is social media important?

    Major social media networks have billions of people visiting their sites on a daily basis. Brands that understand the strengths of social media can boost awareness, drive traffic, engage with future clients, and drive sales amongst many other things.

  • What is a marketing funnel?

    A marketing funnel is a model that represents the stages of a buyer's journey from the first interaction with your brand to a purchase. The funnel metaphor is relevant because it narrows as it progresses, that is the probability of sales decreases at each phase of the funnel.
